principles

// the rules I build by

01
Shipping beats perfecting.
A live product with rough edges teaches more than a perfect product stuck in your head.
02
Design is not decoration.
How something feels is as important as how it works. Motion, spacing, and color carry emotion.
03
AI is a co-pilot, not a replacement.
The builders who use AI creatively will define the next decade. I want to be one of them.
04
The internet is the greatest canvas.
Billions of people scroll past things every day. Building something that makes them stop? That's the game.
05
Taste is a skill.
Technical skills can be copied. Knowing what looks and feels right takes time to cultivate — and it's worth it.
06
Build for yourself first.
The best projects start as scratching your own itch. Genuine obsession shows in the final product.
